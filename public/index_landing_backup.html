Why DropShare?

Designed for Simplicity

Experience a new standard of local file sharing.

Blazing Fast

Forget about upload limits and slow cloud servers. DropShare uses WebRTC to transfer files directly between devices at the speed of your local network. It's instant.

Private & Secure

Your privacy matters. Files travel directly from device to device. They never touch the cloud, they are never stored on any server, and no meta-data is retained.

Cross Platform

Whether you use iPhone, Android, Windows, Mac, or Linux — DropShare works everywhere. Just open the browser. No app installation required.
