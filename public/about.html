What is DropShare?

DropShare is a modern web application that allows you to easily share files between devices on the same network. No setup, no signup, no cloud storage needed - just instant peer-to-peer file sharing.

How It Works

DropShare uses WebRTC technology to establish a direct connection between devices. Your files are transferred directly from one device to another without going through a server, ensuring privacy and speed.

No Setup Required

Just open DropShare in your browser and start sharing files immediately. No account creation or software installation needed.

Privacy Focused

All transfers are peer-to-peer, meaning your files are never stored on our servers. Your data remains private and secure.

Cross-Platform

Works on all modern devices and browsers including smartphones, tablets, and computers running Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, or Android.

Fast Transfers

Direct device-to-device connection means faster transfer speeds than cloud-based services.

How to Use DropShare

Step 1: Connect Devices

Make sure all devices are connected to the same Wi-Fi network or local area network.

Step 2: Open DropShare

Open DropShare on all devices you want to share files between. Just visit the website in your browser.

Step 3: Select Device

You'll see all available devices on the network. Click on the device you want to send files to.

Step 4: Send Files

Select the files you want to share. The recipient will get a notification and can accept the transfer.
